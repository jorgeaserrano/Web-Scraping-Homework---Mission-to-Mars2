Mars Facts

The Equatorial Diameter is 6,792 km, The Polar Diameter is 6,752 km, Mass is 6.39 × 10^23 kg (0.11 Earths), There are 2 Moons - Phobos & Deimos. While the Orbit Distance is 227,943,824 km (1.38 AU), the Orbit Period is 687 days (1.9 years). Lastly, the Surface Temperature is -87 to -5°C' and it was First Recorded in the 2nd millennium BC by Egyptian Astronomers.
